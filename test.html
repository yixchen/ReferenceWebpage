Please refer to http://individual.utoronto.ca/halberstam/Voice_at_Work.pdf for a definition of codeswitching.
The study finds that codeswitching behaviour is associated with the out-group, and may reflect vulnerability they experience at work
as well as disproportionate pressures to conform to in-group norms.
Note that you are not asked to discern between high and low voice pitch, but rather a subtle (3-4%) switch to a male voice frequency mode.
